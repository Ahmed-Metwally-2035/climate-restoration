Analysis

Our Major Conclusions

Calibration

Calibrating temperature sensor before doing the test would increase the accuracy of the results, so the prototype system’s accuracy would increase.

Simulation

Creating the circuit on an online simulator (such as TINKERCAD) would prevent any problems in the connections.

Behavoir of the graph

When the temperature is low there is no loss in Humidity. When it is medium, the RH increases because the intermolecular spaces isn't large enough. When it is high, the system begins to lose humidity.
